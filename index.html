Natural beauty

Situated in the heart of the Lecrin valley, an area of outstanding natural beauty.


Its lush verdant landscape (due to the runoff from sierra Nevada) makes this an ideal destination for anyone looking for a quiet and relaxing holiday.


While in easy reach of the beautiful, historic city of Granada and only half an hours drive from Costa Tropical.


Delicious produce

A working fruit farm, set in 10,000 square meters of gardens and orchards for you to explore. Just a few minutes drive from the village of Melegis, this lovely Andaluz style villa awaits you.

Lots to explore

Planted with fruit trees of every kind - There are lemons, oranges, pears, apricots, cherries, avocados, peach, loquats, grapes and more for you to take your pick and savour directly from the tree.
